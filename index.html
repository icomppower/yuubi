What Yuubi is known for

Two dishes that quietly define the room — one built on patience, one on a spice blend the kitchen won't share.

01

Aged O-Toro — 18-Day

Bluefin tuna belly, dry-aged eighteen days to concentrate its natural sweetness and soften the fat. Served as nigiri or sashimi at the sushi bar.

Nigiri 2pc $15 · Sashimi 3pc $22.50
02

House Curry

A recent addition and already the kitchen's quiet obsession — a slow-built spice base, deep and warming. Ask your server for today's preparation.
